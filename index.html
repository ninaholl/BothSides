    About

    The 21st century poses problems for the sharing of information. There are thousands opinions, articles, and clips circulating around the internet at any given moment, yet the information we come into contact with is self-selected. We tend to share the views of our friends, and therefore our social media accounts consist (mostly) of posts that we agree with. Furthermore, we choose which news outlets to read, listen to, or watch. As a result, it has become increasingly difficult to be exposed to the opposing side of any given perspective.

    This election year is an important one, so it is crucial that all of us engage in a discourse with people with different views. This website aims to get us out of our comfort zones by providing multiple voices in the debate surrounding the 2016 election, so that after hearing both sides, you can make an informed decision.
